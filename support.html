Swinglet Support

Swinglet is a consent-first community platform for adults (18+) exploring ethical non-monogamy, polyamory, and open relationships.

Contact Us

Email: support@swinglet.app

We aim to respond within 48 hours.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account directly from within the App settings.

If you are unable to access your account, email us from your registered email address at support@swinglet.app and we will assist you.

Reporting Abuse or Violations

Swinglet provides in-app reporting and blocking tools.

If you experience harassment, inappropriate behavior, or guideline violations, use the in-app report feature or contact us directly via email.

All reports are reviewed and appropriate action is taken.

Subscriptions & Billing

All subscriptions are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

For billing issues, you may also contact Apple or Google support directly.
