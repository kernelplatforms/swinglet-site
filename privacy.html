Kernel Platforms (“Swinglet”, “we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Swinglet mobile application (“App”), a community platform designed for adults interested in ethical non-monogamy (ENM), polyamory, and open relationship lifestyles.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and moderate personal data when you use the App. The App is currently available in India.

This Policy is intended to comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and other applicable laws.

1. Eligibility

Swinglet is strictly intended for individuals 18 years of age or older.

By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will suspend and permanently remove the account.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to provide and maintain the functionality of the App.

A. Account Information

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (for age confirmation)
  • Gender and relationship status

Email is used for:

  • Account verification
  • One-time passwords (OTP)
  • Password reset
  • Important service communications

We do not access your phone contacts.

B. Profile & Community Information (Optional)

Users may voluntarily provide:

  • Orientation
  • Relationship preferences
  • Interests and lifestyle preferences
  • Kinks and community interests
  • Profile photos and uploaded images
  • Posts, comments, and private messages

Certain profile information (such as orientation or relationship preferences) may be considered sensitive personal data under applicable law and is processed only with your explicit consent.

Content shared within the App may be visible to other users according to your privacy settings.

C. Location Information

We collect general location information to:

  • Enable nearby user discovery
  • Show users within relevant geographic proximity

We do not collect background location data when the App is not actively in use.

D. Device & Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Usage logs
  • Crash and diagnostics data

We use Firebase services (including authentication, analytics, cloud storage, and push notifications) to operate and improve the App.

E. App Permissions

The App may request access to:

  • Camera – to capture profile photos
  • Photo Library – to upload images
  • Location – to enable nearby discovery features
  • Notifications – to receive messages and updates

Permissions can be managed in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable messaging, posting, image sharing, and discovery features
  • Facilitate connections between users
  • Process in-app subscriptions through Apple App Store and Google Play
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or violations of community guidelines
  • Maintain a safe and respectful community environment
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. User-Generated Content & Active Moderation

Swinglet allows users to upload images, create posts, and exchange messages.

Users are responsible for the content they share.

To maintain a safe community environment, Swinglet provides:

  • In-app reporting tools
  • User blocking features
  • Administrative moderation controls

Swinglet actively monitors reported content and reserves the right to:

  • Review content manually
  • Remove content that violates community guidelines
  • Suspend or permanently ban accounts
  • Restrict access to features

We do not permit:

  • Illegal content
  • Non-consensual content
  • Exploitative material
  • Content involving minors
  • Escorting or transactional sexual services

All reports are reviewed and appropriate action is taken at our discretion.

Note: We encourage users to report content or behavior that violates our community guidelines. You can use in-app reporting and blocking tools at any time.
